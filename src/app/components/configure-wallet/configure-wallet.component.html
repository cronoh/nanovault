Before going any further, be 100% certain you have backed up the seed to your current wallet!
Without it, any funds you have will become completely unrecoverable!

When you create a new wallet, a new seed will be generated which can be used to create brand new accounts on the Mikron network. Your Mikron seed is the master key to all of your accounts and any money inside of them!

Make sure you back up your seed, write it down, and keep it incredibly safe! There is NO way to recover it after creation, and all funds inside WILL be lost without it! You have been warned!

If you already have a Mikron wallet, you can import it below. When you import a wallet, none of your existing wallets or accounts are affected, and your seed is not stored anywhere besides in your local client. Use the drop down below to select which type of import you want to use.
